Our Goal

With DATEX, The goal is to supply your business with the best computer chips in the industry, so your computers and machines run quickly and smoothly.

FAQ

Q: Why Are Our Prices So High?

A: In order to make the chips you need for your computers, you need to pay us enough money to buy materials.
Since all of our clients are large businesses and not individuals, the prices are fair.

Q: How Do We Plan To Keep Up With Intel?

A: The purpose of Intel and DATEX are different; Intel makes chips that businesses making computers put in their products for consumer use.
DATEX creates chips that businesses use on their computers to insure nothing goes wrong when, possibly, working on creation and design of a computer that uses Intel.
